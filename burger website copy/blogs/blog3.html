Grill Haven’s Community Day: Giving Back with Great Food!

At Grill Haven, we’re not just passionate about making great food; we’re committed to giving back to the community that has supported us from day one. Next month, we’re excited to host a special Community Day, where we’ll be dedicating a portion of our proceeds to help support local charities focused on hunger relief, food education, and youth programs. From noon until closing, every burger, side, and drink you order will help make a difference in the lives of those in need.Every month at Grill Haven, we’re bringing you a brand-new burger crafted to bring excitement to your taste buds and variety to your meal options. Our chef has worked hard to develop unique burger recipes inspired by flavors from all around the globe, blending fresh, unexpected ingredients with our signature grilling techniques. This month, we’re featuring the “Sriracha Sweetheart” burger—an irresistible combination of a juicy beef patty, spicy sriracha aioli, pickled jalapeños, and a layer of cool, creamy coleslaw, all stacked high on a fresh, toasted bun. It’s a spicy twist we know you’ll crave!

We believe that good food has the power to bring people together, so Community Day will feature a festive atmosphere with live music from local bands, interactive games for families, and exclusive merchandise for those who want to show their Grill Haven pride. The first 50 customers will even receive a complimentary side with their order as a small thank you for their support. Additionally, we’re hosting a donation drive—bring in canned goods or non-perishable items, and you’ll receive a discount on your meal. We’re also collaborating with a few local organizations to offer information on how you can get involved in community projects and initiatives to make a lasting impact.

So, mark your calendar, gather your friends and family, and come enjoy a meal with a purpose at Grill Haven’s Community Day. Together, we can make a positive change while enjoying delicious food and the sense of community that makes Grill Haven feel like home. We can’t wait to celebrate with all of you!
